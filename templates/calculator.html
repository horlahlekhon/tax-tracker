How It Works

Salary (PAYE)

Salaries are taxed at graduated rates from 7% to 24%. Higher salaries mean higher tax rates.

Dividends (WHT)

Dividends are taxed at a flat 10% Withholding Tax rate, which is a final tax.

Optimal Strategy

The optimal split takes some income as salary (lower brackets) and the rest as dividends at the flat 10% rate.
